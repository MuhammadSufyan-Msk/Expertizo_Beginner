CHICKEN BIRYANI

Recipe

Start by marinating 125g of chicken with 1/4 cup yogurt, 1/2 tbsp ginger-garlic paste, 1/4 tsp turmeric powder, 1/4 tsp red chili powder, 1/4 tsp garam masala, 1/4 tsp cumin powder, salt to taste, and 1/2 tbsp lemon juice. Let it sit for at least 30 minutes. Meanwhile, rinse 1/2 cup basmati rice and boil it in 1-1.5 cups of water with 1 bay leaf, 1-2 cloves, 1 cardamom pod, 1 small cinnamon stick, and salt until it’s 70% cooked. Drain and set aside. In a pan, heat 2 tbsp oil or ghee and fry 1/2 thinly sliced onion until golden. Add the marinated chicken and cook for 5-7 minutes, then add 1/2 chopped tomato, 1 slit green chili, 2 tbsp coriander leaves, and 2 tbsp mint leaves. Cook until the chicken is tender and the oil separates. Layer the cooked chicken curry with the partially cooked rice in a small pot. Sprinkle 1/4 tsp biryani masala, 2 tbsp fried onions, and a pinch of saffron soaked in 2 tbsp warm milk. Cover with a tight lid and cook on low heat for 15-20 minutes (dum cooking). Let it rest for 5 minutes, then gently mix and serve hot, garnished with fresh coriander and fried onions. Enjoy your flavorful biryani!

BEEF BIRYANI

Recipe

Start by marinating 125g of beef with 1/4 cup yogurt, 1/2 tbsp ginger-garlic paste, 1/4 tsp turmeric powder, 1/4 tsp red chili powder, 1/4 tsp garam masala, 1/4 tsp cumin powder, salt to taste, and 1/2 tbsp lemon juice. Let it marinate for at least 1 hour (or overnight for better flavor). Meanwhile, rinse 1/2 cup basmati rice and boil it in 1-1.5 cups of water with 1 bay leaf, 1-2 cloves, 1 cardamom pod, 1 small cinnamon stick, and salt until it’s 70% cooked. Drain and set aside. In a pan, heat 2 tbsp oil or ghee and fry 1/2 thinly sliced onion until golden. Add the marinated beef and cook for 10-15 minutes until it’s tender (add a splash of water if needed). Then, add 1/2 chopped tomato, 1 slit green chili, 2 tbsp coriander leaves, and 2 tbsp mint leaves. Cook until the oil separates. Layer the cooked beef curry with the partially cooked rice in a small pot. Sprinkle 1/4 tsp biryani masala, 2 tbsp fried onions, and a pinch of saffron soaked in 2 tbsp warm milk. Cover with a tight lid and cook on low heat for 15-20 minutes (dum cooking). Let it rest for 5 minutes, then gently mix and serve hot, garnished with fresh coriander and fried onions. Enjoy your flavorful beef biryani!

TIKKA BIRYANI

Recipe

Start by marinating 125g of boneless chicken with 2 tbsp yogurt, 1/2 tbsp ginger-garlic paste, 1/4 tsp turmeric powder, 1/4 tsp red chili powder, 1/4 tsp garam masala, 1/4 tsp cumin powder, 1/4 tsp coriander powder, 1/2 tbsp lemon juice, 1/2 tbsp oil, and salt to taste. Let it sit for 30 minutes. Cook the marinated chicken on a pan or grill until it’s fully cooked and slightly charred, then set aside. Rinse 1/2 cup basmati rice and boil it in 1-1.5 cups of water with 1 bay leaf, 1-2 cloves, 1 cardamom pod, 1 small cinnamon stick, and salt until it’s 70% cooked. Drain and set aside. In a small pot, layer the cooked chicken tikka at the bottom, add the partially cooked rice on top, and sprinkle 1/4 tsp biryani masala, 2 tbsp fried onions, and a pinch of saffron soaked in 2 tbsp warm milk. Cover with a tight lid and cook on low heat for 15-20 minutes (dum cooking). Let it rest for 5 minutes, then gently mix and serve hot, garnished with fresh coriander, mint leaves, and fried onions. Enjoy your aromatic and flavorful Tikka Biryani
